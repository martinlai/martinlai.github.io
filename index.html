About

I am a Computer Engineering student at the University of Waterloo. Many people ask me why I decided to pursue a career in the technology industry. Like many people, I have considered how amazing it would be to change the world; to be ambitious enough to strive for a goal that many speak about but rarely achieve. Through my experiences, I have seen that technology can reach an unfathomable amount of people and accomplish an equally unimaginable number of things. All of this is in reach for a motivated individual.

Fortunately, being motivated just means doing something I already love - making something and learning from my challenges and failures along the way.
